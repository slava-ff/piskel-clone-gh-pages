  • Tools: pen, stroke, rectangle, eraser, color picker, paint canvas, colorswap, "rainbow", lighten/darken, two colors selecting (left/right mouse click painting).

  • Frame management: add, delete, clone, activate any of them.
  • Preview: animation preview playing 1-24 fps, fullscreen mode.
  • Export: to filesystem as .apng and .gif, to Google Drive as .apng
  • Log In: via google account
  • Show cursor coordinates
